What We Do

We enhance the systems your business already relies on

We take your existing interfaces, CRMs, Revit models and management systems — everything your team uses every day — and supercharge them with AI. No disruption. No starting over. Just smarter, faster, more capable versions of what you already have.

How we do it

Our approach is built around one principle — enhance, don't replace. Here's what that looks like in practice.

We Work With What You Have

Your team won't stop using the tools they rely on — and they shouldn't have to. We plug directly into your existing interfaces, workflows and platforms, enhancing them with AI rather than replacing them.

CRM & System Integration

From Salesforce to HubSpot, Revit to bespoke management systems — if it's programmatic, we can connect AI to it. We bridge the gap between your current systems and the intelligence they've been missing.

AI-Enhanced Capabilities

We layer powerful AI solutions on top of your existing stack — automating responses, surfacing insights, streamlining decisions — so your business moves faster without the disruption of starting over.

Supplier & API Connectivity

We build seamless API integrations that connect your suppliers, partners and internal systems, giving AI the data it needs to work intelligently across your entire operation.

Rapid Time to Value

Because we enhance rather than replace, our solutions are live faster. No lengthy migrations, no retraining your team on new software — just immediate AI gains on top of familiar ground.

Measurable Outcomes

Every integration we build is tied to a business outcome. Faster enquiry handling, smarter lead routing, reduced manual effort — we measure what matters and make sure you see it.

“Your team won't change how they work. They'll just be amazed at what they can now achieve.”

— The Amatatek approach
